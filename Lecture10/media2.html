About Chatrapati

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India123. He was born in Shivneri Fort in Maharashtra on February 19, 1630145. Shivaji carved out his own independent kingdom from the declining Adilshahi sultanate of Bijapur, which eventually became the genesis of the Maratha Empire23. He is considered to be one of the greatest warriors of his time and is often regarded as the father of the Indian Navy5.
